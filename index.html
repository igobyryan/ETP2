EcoTipping Points

A practical toolkit for facilitating organizations working on community-scale ecological restoration, grounded in systems thinking and field experience across the rural Global South.

01

Systems Thinking

A four-part framework for understanding how ecological decline happens and how to reverse it: recognize the pattern, map the causes, find the levers, tip the system back toward health.
02

Ingredients for Success

Ten conditions that separated restoration efforts that lasted from those that didn't. Use these to assess what's present, what needs building, and what to work around.
03

Community Sessions

Facilitation guides for leading a community through planning and implementing a successful restoration effort.
