Community Sessions

Structured facilitation guides for NGO practitioners running community workshops that turn systems understanding into collective action.

The systems thinking tool helps a community understand their situation. These sessions are where they do something about it.

Map It and Reverse It are structured facilitation guides for NGO practitioners running community workshops. Each session builds on the last: Map It produces a shared diagram of how the community's ecological and social problems connect and reinforce each other. Reverse It uses that diagram to find where an intervention could actually shift the system.

Both guides are designed to be run by a facilitating NGO with moderate facilitation experience. They work alongside the EcoTipping Points systems thinking framework, not as a replacement for local knowledge: the community's own understanding of their situation is what drives the process.

Map It

A community mapping session produces a shared diagram showing how ecological and social problems connect and reinforce each other, and where a change might find traction.

Reverse It
